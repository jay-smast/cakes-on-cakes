Who's this guy?

Meet Jay, the Midwest baker with a knack for making food taste as good as it looks! With roots in Amish baking, Jay's love for creating delicious and visually stunning treats started early in his grandma Fanny's kitchen.

After finding great joy in baking countless cakes for his friends' birthdays over the last few years, Jay decided to share his delectable creations with the world.

Art is fleeting - and what better way to hasten the demise of something stunning then to devour it! As Grandma Fanny always said, “You eat with your eyes.”
